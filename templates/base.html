Hello and Welcome to The Medicine Cabinet! This website was created to showcase an e-commerce site built in Django.

The Medicine Cabinet is an online store where users can buy medicine, such as pain killers, first aid items, medical equipment and topical treatments.

The Medicine Cabinet features a Stripe API which allows easy payments to be made quickly. Items are priced by BitCoin, but do not reflect real world prices, due to rapid fluctuation.

Users can login to their profile which stores their cart, even after leaving the page.


Q: Can I purchase any of these items?

A: No, please do not submit any legitimate payment details to this website.

Q: What is sold here?

A: Medicine and Medical equipment. While some of the items list can be used for recreational purposes, I advise against it.

Q: Can I sell my own medication here?

A: We do not buy medication from individual resellers. I advise against selling your medication unless absolutely necessary.
